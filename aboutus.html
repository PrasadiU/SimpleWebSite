"Student Admission information system for Dhamma school in Lellagoda. “Sri Saranankara Dhamma School” has located in Lellagoda. In dhamma school has around 120 students from grade 1 to 11. When a student arrives at the dhamma school, the office gives him an application form to fill out. The applications are about the student’s index number, Name, Address, Phone Number, Grade. Its student registration system has computerized as manual system."
